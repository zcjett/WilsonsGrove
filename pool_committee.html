 Guest Passes

The limit on guests is four (4) guests per household per visit. There is no overall yearly limit on the number of guests, and there is no charge for guest passes. Guests must be accompanied by an adult homeowner at all times.

 Prohibited Items

The following items are prohibited on pool grounds: glass containers, illegal drugs, animals (except support dogs), smoking, gum, external chairs, diving, etc.

 Food

Food can be consumed 10 feet from poolside. No grills or gas or electric cooking devices. Ice cream is provided in the pool garage free of charge via homeowner donations.
