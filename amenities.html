Amenities

Wilson's Grove is an exceptional community with amenities such as an outdoor pool, walking paths, tennis and basketball courts, a clubhouse with a fitness center, a playground and tot lot and more, all ensuring a vibrant and active lifestyle. The community hosts movie nights, children's activities and so much more! Convenient and easy access to Washington DC, Annapolis, Baltimore and Ft Meade, alike. Its location is simply ideal, with easy access to I-97, 32, 301, Route 50, BWI, Waugh Chapel Towne Center and wonderful restaurants.

 Pool

Wilson's Grove residents enjoy a community pool that provides entertainment for all ages. The pool is open from Memorial Day to Labor Day and is staffed with lifeguards at all times. Kids can enjoy a small kiddie pool or a massive 3 foot deep pool with a large fountain that provides hours of entertainment. Adults can enjoy 3 swimming lanes, as well as ample chairs, tables and umbrellas to relax in the sun. The pool house comes with bathrooms, showers, and a small indoor area with a TV and ice cream free to all homeowners.

 Gym

Looking for a private gym for your weekly exercise regimen? Wilson's Grove has that too! Residents enjoy a private gym available only to WG homeowners via restricted key fob access. The gym had treadmills, ellipticals, dumbbells, and many machines (bench, leg extension, curl and press, bench, rows, etc). The gym includes a TV with cable, restrooms, and free wifi.

 Playground, Basketball and Tennis Courts

At Wilson's Grove, you can always see children outdoors and enjoying the community playground, which has 3 slides, monkey bars, and plenty of space to run and play. Adjacent to the playground is a basketball court and a tennis court that hosts weekly pickleball games. The tennis court is able to be reserved by homeowners.

 Walking Paths

Despite being located 2 miles from the Waugh Chapel Towne Center, meandering along the Wilson's Grove walking paths transports its residents to a wooded wilderness rarely enjoyed in the Baltimore / Washington area. With over a mile of walking paths throughout the community in addition to the 3+ miles of sidewalks, WG has everything to keep its residents active and healthy. Just watch out for the wildlife!
