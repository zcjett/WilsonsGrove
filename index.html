Wilson's Grove

Gambrills, Maryland

Welcome home! Wilson's Grove is an exceptional community with amenities such as an outdoor pool, walking paths, tennis and basketball courts, a clubhouse with a fitness center, a playground and tot lot and so much more.

Take an Aerial Tour!

Community Details

Established in 2015

Homes built between 2015 and 2019, with a Home Owners Association that has ensured the community maintains its beauty for the past decade.

183 Home Sites

All single family homes with >15 different models from two different builders to ensure aesthetic diversity across the community.

Crofton School District

Wilson's Grove children attend Nantucket Elementary, Crofton Middle, and Crofton High School, each with a rating of 8+ from GreatSchools.org.

Public Utilities

All Wilson's Grove homes have public water and public sewer access, along with the option for natural gas appliances.
