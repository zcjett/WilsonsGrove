 2025 Accomplishments

2024 was a year of transition. The Board formally moved away from the management company that had run the community since the first house was built, and moved away from many vendors that were initially chosen by the original management company. In addition, the Board made processes quicker and cheaker (aka key fobs), made the community more beautiful (parking lot reseal, increased focus on landscpaing), and made the community safer (dead tree removal). 2025 will be more of the same - the Board will continue to analyze vendor performance and move to new vendors where necessary, while also increasing efforts to improve landscaping and finalzing a major pool project.
