 Santa Visits Wilson's Grove!

Join us on December 11 for a festive and fun-filled evening at the WG Holiday Celebration! Bring the whole family to enjoy a variety of holiday activities:

  • Cookies and Milk: Indulge in delicious cookies and sip on milk while mingling with neighbors.
  • Crafts Corner: Let your creativity shine with fun, holiday-themed crafts for all ages.
  • Pictures with Santa: Don’t miss your chance to capture the magic of the season with a special photo with Santa Claus himself!

As we spread holiday cheer, we’re also giving back to the community. Please bring non-perishable food items to donate to the Anne Arundel County Food Bank. Your generosity will help make the season brighter for families in need. We can’t wait to celebrate with you and create lasting memories together. See you there!
